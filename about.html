Mendon House

About

Dick Moll’s, the predecessor to Mendon House, was my grandfathers first restaurant. In the early 90’s he chose to close the original restaurant and relocate to Rochester, NY. In 1992 the doors to Mendon House opened. Service ran from Tuesday through Sunday, 4pm until the last patrons left for the night for 11 years when he and my grandmother retired.
