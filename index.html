Problem Statement

Since Mendon House closed in the early 2000’s, the recipes from my grandfather’s
restaurant have been unavailable for anyone outside of our family.

With the passing of my grandfather 2 years ago, and since gaining possession of
all his recipes, now feels like the perfect time to organize and better preserve
his recipes and the family history.

Mendon House: The Cookbook is an exploration of food as heritage by cataloging these
recipes into an accessible and approachable method for any level cook.
