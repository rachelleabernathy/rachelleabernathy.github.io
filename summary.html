Mendon House

Summary

In the 80’s, the idea of owning a restaurant was just a dream for my grandparents. Their restaurants turned into second careers, and while it wasn’t their intention, these restaurants impacted dozens of employees and generations of family that followed them.

When my grandfather passed two years ago, nearly two decades after the doors to Mendon House closed, approximately half of the attendees of his funeral were former employees. Seeing people gather to remember him emphasized the impact that my grandfather had, and made it that more important to take the recipes that had been left to my family and preserve them.

While I hope that one day these recipes are once again accessible for the general population, what matters is that his legacy is documented and conserved for any further descendants. Ultimately, that is what I hope to have accomplished with this project.
